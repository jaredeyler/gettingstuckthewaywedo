My name is Jared, and this is Getting Stuck, The Way We Do.

This is a project I designed - built from the ground up as part of my graduate work, and shaped continuously as I live day-to-day.

Since the start of 2026, I've been keeping a daily journal, cataloging my emotions, behaviors, and patterns - essentially creating a paper trail of myself. Not to expose myself, but to try and better understand who I am and who I want to be. To see where I get stuck, and what it means to actually move.

Getting Stuck, The Way We Do is a research project, but it's a personal one. You'll find interviews with psychologists and people I genuinely admire - people who have something to say about identity, how our brains work, and what it means to change. And alongside that research, you'll find me - my journals, my processes, and my own attempts to get unstuck.
